## Key Design Principles

### For Children
- **Visual First**: Large icons, emojis, colors
- **Gamification**: Points, stars, badges, rewards
- **Positive Reinforcement**: Celebrate all progress
- **Age-Appropriate**: Content filtered by age
- **Simple Navigation**: Big buttons, clear paths
- **Engaging Feedback**: Animations, sounds, celebrations

### For Caregivers
- **Overview-Focused**: See all children at a glance
- **Data-Driven**: Charts, stats, trends
- **Actionable Insights**: Recommendations and alerts
- **Easy Management**: Simple profile creation/editing
- **Progress Tracking**: Historical data and comparisons
- **Peace of Mind**: Know what children are learning

### For Therapists
- **Clinical View**: Professional, detailed information
- **Multi-Child Management**: Efficient workflow for many children
- **Pattern Recognition**: Identify trends and concerns
- **Session Tools**: Notes, goals, scheduling
- **Reporting**: Export data for records
- **Collaboration**: Share insights with caregivers (if enabled)

---

**This visual flow represents the refined EmoLor architecture with unified child-caregiver interface and separate therapist access.**
